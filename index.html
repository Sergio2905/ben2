about BenDog

Story

In 2017, Anatoly Yakovenko was working on a new blockchain project — Solana. To nail it, he needed an unconventional advisor and discovered BenDog

His simple but timely and inspiring "Yeahppps" helped Anatoly to take the plunge. As a result, Solana became a massive success, and BenDog — a significant member of its community.

Now the famous dog is inviting its friends to the ecosystem, named after him, Benhalla. Playful and creative, together they are shaping next-gen blockchain games. Join the party and have fun!
